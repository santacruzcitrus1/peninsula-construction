WHAT WE BUILD IN PACIFIC GROVE

Every Type of ADU, Done Right

Peninsula Construction builds all five ADU types in Pacific Grove. We'll help you determine which type maximizes your property's potential.

🏠

Detached ADU

A fully separate structure in your backyard. Maximum privacy and rental value. Custom designed to your lot.

🏡

Attached ADU

Connected to your main home but with a separate entrance. Cost-effective with shared wall construction.

🏢

Garage Conversion

The most affordable ADU path. We convert your existing garage into a fully permitted living space.

🚪

Junior ADU (JADU)

Up to 500 sq ft carved out of your existing home. Lower cost, faster permits, great for in-law suites.

⬆️

Two-Story ADU

Maximize square footage on smaller lots by building up. Ideal for Pacific Grove properties with limited yard space.
