Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building an ADU in Santa Cruz County.

Ask Us a Question
How much does an ADU cost in Santa Cruz County? +
Our ADUs range from $135,000 to $300,000 all-in, depending on size, type, and complexity. A garage conversion is typically on the lower end, while a detached two-story ADU is higher. We provide transparent pricing upfront with no hidden fees.
How long does it take to build an ADU? +
Most ADU projects take 6–9 months from the initial consultation to final inspection. The permit process typically takes 2–4 months, and construction takes 3–5 months depending on complexity. We'll give you a detailed timeline at your free consultation.
Do you handle permits and design? +
Yes — we handle the entire process including architectural drawings, city permits, utility connections, and all inspections. You don't need to coordinate with anyone else. We're truly full-process from day one to keys in hand.
What types of ADUs do you build? +
We build detached ADUs, attached ADUs, garage conversions, JADUs (Junior ADUs), and two-story ADUs. We also offer three standard design options for 2-bedroom units under 750 sq ft — great for keeping costs predictable.
Is financing available? +
Yes — we offer financing options up to $100,000 with every estimate. We can walk you through ADU-specific financing products like HELOCs, construction loans, and renovation loans to find what works best for your situation.
