Our Story

Meet Richard Rodriguez

Richard Rodriguez founded Peninsula Construction over 30 years ago in Watsonville, California. What started as a small general contracting operation has grown into one of the most trusted construction companies in Santa Cruz County — built entirely on referrals and repeat clients.

Richard obtained his CSLB General Building license (#723364) in 1996 and has never stopped building. Over the years, he's completed hundreds of projects across the Central Coast — from garage conversions and kitchen remodels to full ADU builds and ground-up residential development.

As a real estate investor himself for over 12 years, Richard brings a unique perspective to every ADU project: he doesn't just build your unit, he helps you understand the return on your investment.

"I've been building in this community for over 30 years. My reputation is everything to me. Every project gets the same care I'd give my own home."

— Richard Rodriguez, Owner
